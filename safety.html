Committed to Safety and OHSA Compliance

At Kamato Rigging Specialists, safety is not a checkbox — it's a mindset. We strictly comply with the Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA) to ensure that every team member, client, and project site remains protected and incident-free. Our rigging operations are grounded in rigorous training, pre-task hazard assessments, and continuous monitoring.

Risk Assessments

All projects begin with thorough hazard identification and risk analysis before work starts.

PPE Enforcement

We strictly enforce use of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) on every site, every task, every time.

Emergency Preparedness

Our staff are trained in emergency response procedures, including overhead crane incidents and shaft rescues.

Compliance Audits

Routine internal and external audits ensure we meet or exceed OHSA standards across all operations.

Toolbox Talks

Daily safety briefings ensure awareness of site-specific risks and reinforce safe behavior.

Incident Reporting

Zero tolerance for cover-ups. All incidents are logged, reviewed, and used to improve future safety.

Our Safety Practices

  • Strict adherence to OHSA Section 8 and 9 responsibilities
  • Regular load testing of slings, chain blocks, and overhead cranes
  • EMTs and visual inspections of winder ropes and lifting gear
  • Tagging and color coding of all lifting equipment
  • Continuous safety training for all riggers and logistics crew
  • Development of Job-Specific Risk Assessments (JSRA)
  • Site Safety Files and Method Statements for all projects
  • Weekly site inspections and leadership walkabouts
