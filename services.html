Our Services

Explore the diverse range of services we provide to support your operations and growth.

Training & Support

Training & Support

We provide skills development and mentorship services tailored for companies and individuals. Our training ensures that rigging personnel meet safety and operational standards, including proper lifting practices, hazard awareness, and equipment usage. We partner with local mines and businesses to deliver long-term, project-specific development.

Mining Projects

Mining Projects & Equipment

Kamato provides professional rigging services for mining operations including: winder ropes, conveyors, chairlift systems, box fronts, shaft equipment, and underground materials. With decades of experience, we specialize in rope splicing, load handling, and safe material movement in vertical shafts and confined mining spaces.

Events & Management

Events & Management

We manage corporate events, team-building sessions, seminars, and workshops with full logistical support. Our services include venue sourcing, transport coordination, safety planning, and scheduling. We take care of the details so you can focus on the outcome.

Consulting & Recruitment

Consulting & Recruitment

We help empower communities through employment and training opportunities. Our recruitment services target youth, women, and unemployed adults to prepare them for industrial roles. We assess candidate readiness and match them to suitable opportunities in mining, logistics, and project work.

Rigging & Logistics

Rigging & Logistics

We are specialists in heavy machinery rigging, relocation, and logistics. Our services include:

  • Relocating oversized equipment
  • Lifting heavy materials using cranes and hoists
  • Transporting sensitive or complex cargo
  • On-site logistics planning
  • Equipment installation and decommissioning

Each task is executed with safety, precision, and full compliance to regulations.
