About Us

Kamato Rigging Specialists is a privately owned, 70% Black-owned company established in 2024 by three seasoned Riggers: Mr. Mathews Mokalanyane, Mr. Kallie Brunning, and Mr. Isaac Landela. Together, they bring over 20 years of experience in heavy lifting, rope work, equipment tagging, NDTs, and load testing in mining and industrial environments.

We are based in Rustenburg, North West Province, South Africa, and operate across the greater Bojanala district. Kamato is proud to hold a B-BBEE Certificate for Exempted Micro Enterprises.

Our business model is built around sustainable partnerships with labor, government sectors, and mining houses. Our team includes experts in project management, procurement, stakeholder relations, enterprise development, recruitment, and more.

We aim to penetrate mechanized and conventional underground mining through rigging solutions that address issues like cage falls, improper rope maintenance, and unsafe practices. We actively train and mentor new riggers in safe, industry-compliant methods to prevent human and production losses.

Our Services

  • Training Facilitation & Support: Tailored mentorship and training for mines and businesses.
  • Mining Projects & Equipment: Rigging services on winders, conveyors, chairlift ropes, box fronts, and more.
  • Events & Management: Organizing logistics for conferences, team building, and corporate events.
  • Consulting & Recruitment: Developing opportunities for youth, women, and unemployed individuals.
  • Heavy Machinery Rigging: Rigging and relocating large-scale industrial equipment.
  • Material Handling & Lifting: Safe and efficient crane and hoist operations.
  • Specialized Transport & Logistics: Custom transportation for oversized and sensitive loads.
  • Project Logistics Management: Full-service on-site planning and execution.
  • Installation & Decommissioning: Placement and removal of heavy industrial equipment.
